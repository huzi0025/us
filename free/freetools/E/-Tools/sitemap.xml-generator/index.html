What is a Sitemap.xml and Why Is It Important?

    
A sitemap.xml file is an XML file that lists all the important pages of your website, helping search engines like Google, Bing, and others to crawl your website more intelligently and efficiently. It informs search engines about the structure of your site and the URLs available for crawling.


    
How Does a Sitemap Help SEO?

    
Search engines use sitemaps to discover new and updated content faster. A well-structured sitemap improves the chances of your pages getting indexed promptly, which can positively impact your search rankings and organic traffic.
