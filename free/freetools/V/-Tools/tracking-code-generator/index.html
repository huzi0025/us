What Is Scroll Tracking?

    
Scroll tracking allows website owners and marketers to monitor how far down users scroll on a webpage. This is a powerful way to measure content engagement and understand user behavior patterns.

Scroll Tracking vs Heatmaps

    
While heatmaps provide a visual overview, scroll tracking offers precise percentages of user scrolls, making it ideal for quantifiable tracking in analytics tools.
