What is a Link Analyzer Tool?

A link analyzer tool helps webmasters and SEO professionals analyze the internal and external links within a web page or HTML document. It shows the number of outbound links, internal references, and anchor texts. Understanding link distribution is key to on-page SEO optimization.

Why Use a Link Analyzer Tool?

Links are one of the most important ranking factors in search engine optimization. A well-structured internal linking strategy improves crawlability and distributes authority throughout your site. On the other hand, too many outbound links or broken links can hurt your rankings. A link analyzer helps identify and optimize these issues.
