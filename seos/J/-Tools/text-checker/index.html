Why Image Alt Text Is Crucial for SEO and Accessibility

  
Alt text (alternative text) is used to describe images on your website to both search engines and users who rely on screen readers. Proper alt attributes improve your website's search engine optimization by giving context to images and enhance accessibility for visually impaired users.


  
How Missing or Empty Alt Text Affects Your Website

  
Images without alt attributes or with empty alt text may be ignored by search engines, potentially reducing your site's SEO effectiveness. Moreover, accessibility tools will not be able to convey meaningful information to users, leading to a poor user experience.

Best Practices for Writing Image Alt Text

  
    
    
  • Describe the image content clearly and concisely.
    • 
    
  • Keep alt text relevant to the image and surrounding content.
    • 
    
  • Avoid keyword stuffing in alt attributes.
    • 
    
  • If the image is purely decorative, use empty alt text alt="" to indicate that.
