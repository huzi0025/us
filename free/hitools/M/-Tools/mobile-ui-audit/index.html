Frequent UX Gaps & Quick Wins

Across 200+ audits, five pitfalls dominate:

  • Invisible error states. 38 % of apps show generic toast messages that vanish before reading.
  • Tap target crowding. Ads jammed next to nav icons cause 22 % mis-taps in our tests.
  • Keyboard obstruction. Forms without scrollIntoView() hide the CTA under the virtual keyboard.
  • Slow first render. Heavy fonts block FCP; switching to variable fonts cuts 200 ms on 3G.
  • Gesture ambiguity. Swiping conflicts with OS back gesture, leading to accidental exits.

North-Star Metrics After the Audit

Post-audit, teams gravitate toward three golden indicators:

  1. Task-completion rate. Percentage of sessions that reach success screen.
  2. Time to first action. Seconds from load to first meaningful tap.
  3. Tap precision score. Ratio of intended taps to total taps in journey.

FAQ: Troubleshooting Mobile UX

Do dark-mode designs need a separate audit?

Yes. Contrast, elevation, and brand colors behave differently. Run the checklist in both modes.

How often should we audit?

Quarterly for high-velocity startups; at major version releases for mature apps.

What if checklist items conflict with brand guidelines?

Prioritize clarity over aesthetics; negotiate compromises in design tokens.

Is 120 Hz refresh mandatory?

No, but jitter on pro-motion screens feels jarring. Optimize frame pacing, not just FPS.
