Best Practices for UTM Tagging

Follow these battle-tested guidelines to keep your analytics pristine:

  1. Adopt a naming convention. Standardize lower-case, underscored, human-readable labels such as spring_sale instead of SPRINGSALE or springSale.
  2. Never use spaces. A space is encoded as %20, which clutters reports. Stick to dashes or underscores.

Never. UTMs on internal links overwrite the original acquisition data, corrupting attribution. Use GA4 events instead.
