What Are Twitter Cards?

Twitter Cards allow you to attach rich photos, videos and media experiences to Tweets that drive traffic to your website. By adding a few lines of HTML to your webpages, users who share your content on Twitter will see a ‚Äúcard‚Äù that includes images, descriptions, and other relevant data.

Types of Twitter Cards

  • Summary Card: A small card with a title, description, and thumbnail image.
  • Summary Large Image Card: Similar to Summary Card but with a prominently displayed large image.
  • App Card: A card that provides a direct download link to a mobile app.
  • Player Card: Enables embedding of audio or video media players within the card.
