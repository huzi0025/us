What Is a GDPR Cookie Consent Banner?

A GDPR Cookie Consent Banner is a notification displayed on websites to inform visitors about the use of cookies and obtain their explicit consent, in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This banner allows users to accept or reject cookies, thereby protecting their privacy rights.

Why Your Website Needs a GDPR Cookie Consent Banner

Under GDPR and ePrivacy Directive, websites targeting EU residents must inform users about cookie usage and obtain consent before storing cookies on their devices. Failure to comply can lead to hefty fines and reputational damage. A clear, user-friendly cookie consent banner is the easiest way to stay compliant and build trust with your visitors.

  • Make sure users can easily accept or reject cookies
  • Do not set non-essential cookies before user consent
  • Provide a clear and comprehensive cookie policy page
  • Keep detailed records of user consents
  • Regularly update your cookie practices and banner text
