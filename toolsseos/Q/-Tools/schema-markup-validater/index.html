Frequently Asked Questions

Does Schema markup directly improve rankings?

Not as a stand-alone ranking factor, but it strongly influences CTR by enabling rich results. Higher engagement signals can indirectly lift rankings over time.

How often should I update structured data?

Whenever page content changes materially ‚Äî price, availability, ratings, dates. Stale markup can trigger manual actions or cause search engines to ignore your data.

Can I include multiple Schema types on one page?

Yes, but nest them logically and avoid contradicting information. An Article page can also embed FAQPage or BreadcrumbList.

Is Microdata still supported?

It works, but JSON-LD is preferred for flexibility and cleaner HTML. Mixed paradigms complicate maintenance.

Will invalid markup hurt my site?

Usually Google ignores invalid items, but persistent widespread errors may downgrade trust. Keep error rates below 1 % of indexed URLs.
