Facebook Data Deletion Request

If you want to delete your data from our app, please follow the steps below:

  1. Go to your Facebook account settings.
  2. Select Apps and Websites.
  3. Find our app and click Remove.

Alternatively, you can request manual data deletion by emailing us at admin@tools.histream.me.
