Why Optimize Your Images?

How Does Image Compression Work?

Image compression algorithms remove redundant or less important data from an image file, thereby reducing its size. There are two main types:

  • Lossless Compression: Reduces file size without losing any quality. Perfect for PNGs and graphics where every pixel counts.
  • Lossy Compression: Achieves higher compression rates by sacrificing some image detail, usually imperceptible to the human eye. Commonly used with JPEG and WebP formats.

While our tool automates much of the optimization process, here are some expert tips to get the best results:
