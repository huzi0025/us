What is a QR Code?

QR codes (Quick Response codes) are two-dimensional barcodes that store information, such as URLs, contact details, or text. They are widely used for marketing, mobile payments, product tracking, and more due to their ease of scanning and versatility.

How to Use Our QR Code Generator

Customization Options

Though this basic generator creates black-and-white QR codes for universal compatibility, advanced users can enhance them with colors, logos, and shapes using more sophisticated tools. However, simplicity often ensures maximum scan reliability.

Benefits of Using QR Codes in Your Business

Incorporating QR codes into your marketing and product materials can improve customer engagement, provide easy access to digital content, and facilitate contactless interactions. From restaurant menus to event tickets, QR codes offer convenience and interactivity.

SEO and QR Codes

Using QR codes linked to your website URLs can indirectly support SEO by increasing user engagement, improving brand recognition, and driving mobile traffic. Make sure your landing pages are optimized for mobile users for the best experience.

Technical Details Behind QR Codes

QR codes encode data using black modules arranged in a square grid on a white background. Error correction is built in, allowing recovery of up to 30% of data if damaged. The encoding follows international standards making QR codes universally readable.
